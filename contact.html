I love hearing from you. Whether it be a note in the mailbox, an email or a simple comment on a blog post, please write! I'd love to hear your stories about life. Funny stories, stories about people you love and stories about kitchen mishaps, all of it. I'll mostly share the kitchen mishaps with my dad to encourage him in his own kitchen endeavors, but nonetheless, I'll enjoy it. One time, I taught a class of fifteen elementary schoolers AND their parents how to make biscuits and I put a cup of salt in the dough instead of a teaspoon. No one noticed till we served the biscuits for the family meal at the end of the class. Well, as you might imagine, when they took the first bite -- they noticed. Nonetheless, we all make mistakes and sometimes the story after the fact was well worth the embarrassing moment. Send on the stories and I'll laugh and enjoy them with you.
