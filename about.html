Hi, my name is Laura Hart

I am a Kentucky girl with deep family ties, Michigan farm roots and a Southern ideal for sipping peach tea on a front porch swing. Sometimes I am serious. Sometimes I am silly. Sometimes I am focused. Sometimes I am fun. I bake and play through life with sweet people I love. As a follower of Jesus, I hope both my heart and this website treasure all that is true, noble, lovely... and tasty.

And in the meantime, I hope our times around the kitchen table honor Him who made us.

Sending sweets and sunny days,

Laura
